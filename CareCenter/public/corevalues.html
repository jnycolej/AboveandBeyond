About Us

Welcome to Above and Beyond Care Center

Core Values

  1. Compassion: We approach every interaction with empathy, understanding the unique needs and aspirations of each individual under our care.
  2. Respect: We honor th dignity and worth of every participant, fostering an atmosphere of mutual respect and inclusivity.
  3. Empowerment: We empower our participants to actively engage in meaningful activities that nurture their interests, skills, and passions
  4. Community: We cultivate a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our participants, families, and state creating strong and vibrant community.
  5. Innovation: We continuously seek innovative ways to enhance the quality of life for our participants, adapting to their changing needs and preferences.

Our Promise:

At Above and Beyond Care Center, we are dedicated to building meaningful days for our Adult Day Care population. Our expert staff, personalized care plans, and enriching activities combine to create an environment where each participant's unique journey is honored and celebrated. Through laughter, learning, and connection, we strive to make every day a source of inspiration, purpose, and fulfillment.
