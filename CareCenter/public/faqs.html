General Questions

  • What is Above and Beyond Care Center?

    Above and Beyond Care Center is an adult daycare center that provides exceptional care and support to adults betwen 18-65 with disabilities.

  • What is the mission of Above and Beyond Care Center?

    Our mission is to create a warm and nurturing environment while promotinng independence, social connections, and overall well-being for our participants.

Services and Programs

  • What services do you offer at Above and Beyond Care Center?

    We offer a range of services, including personal care assistance, recreational activities, therapy services, health monitoring, nutritious meals, and more.

  • What types of recreational activities are available?

    Our recreational activities include social games, arts and crafts, group exercises, educational workshops, and outings

  • Do you provide therapy services?

    Yes, we offer occupational therapy, physical therapy, and speech therapy to support participants' developmental and functional goals.

Eligibility & Enrollment

  • Who is eligible to participate in the program?

    Above and Beyond Care Center is open to adults between 18065 with disabilities
